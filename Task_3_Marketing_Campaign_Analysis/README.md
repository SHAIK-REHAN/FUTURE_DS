# Task 3: Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze marketing campaign performance by evaluating key metrics such as impressions, clicks, conversion rate, cost, and ROI to derive actionable business insights.

## Dataset
**Source:** Kaggle – Marketing Campaign Performance Dataset

## Tools & Libraries
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

## Key Performance Metrics (KPIs)
- **CTR (Click Through Rate)** = Clicks / Impressions  
- **Conversion Rate** = Conversion Rate (provided in dataset)  
- **ROI (Return on Investment)** = (Revenue – Cost) / Cost  

## Analysis Performed
- Channel-wise performance comparison
- Target audience ROI and engagement analysis
- Identification of high-performing marketing segments
- Visualization of ROI and conversion trends

## Key Insights
- Certain channels consistently deliver higher ROI.
- Specific target audiences show better engagement and conversion efficiency.
- Marketing effectiveness varies significantly across campaign segments.

## Recommendations
- Allocate higher budgets to channels and audiences with consistently high ROI.
- Reduce or optimize spending on low-performing campaigns.
- Use data-driven targeting to improve conversion efficiency and profitability.

## Conclusion
This analysis demonstrates how data-driven marketing decisions can significantly improve campaign performance. By focusing on high-ROI channels and audiences, organizations can optimize marketing spend and maximize revenue growth.
